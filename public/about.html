About Me

I am a Regional Manager at LearnWell, a company that provides educational services to students receiving medical or other out-of-school care. Our main goal is to ensure students still have a connection to their typical school environment during this time . My role is to ensure that the medical facilities and teachers receive all the support and tools they need to thrive.

When I am not working, I enjoy learning about the latest and greatest technologies while also maintaining my Philly sports fandom. I would also include that I have learned to pick up golfing as a weekend getaway, but my handicap would suggest that is more than just a work in progress. Ultimately, though, if you have a story to tell, I am always one who is happy to listen.
